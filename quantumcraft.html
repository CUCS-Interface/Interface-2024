QUANTUM CRAFT

AI Image Generator


DESCRIPTION
Embark on an exhilarating journey of knowledge and creativity as teams delve into the realms of code snippets. With just 2 minutes per question, teams race against the clock, unlocking keywords with each correct choice. The stakes rise as these keywords become the building blocks for an imaginative prompt, transformed into a visual masterpiece using the cutting-edge Adobe Firefly AI model. Be prepared for a thrilling fusion of intellect and innovation, where every decision shapes the path to victory in this dynamic competition.


Duration : 25min
No. of Rounds : 1
No. of Participants in a Team : 2 participants


RULES

  • Each team tackles ten multiple-choice output questions related to DSA, DBMS, and OS, with each option linked to a keyword.
  • Teams have 2 minutes to solve each question, receiving the correct keyword upon correct selection and a wrong keyword upon wrong selection.
  • After all questions, teams get 5 minutes to engineer a prompt using the collected keywords and generate an image with the Adobe Firefly AI model.
  • If a team fails to select an option within 2 minutes, they proceed to the next question without the associated keyword.
  • In the absence of a correct image, marks are awarded based on the number of correct answers in the MCQ.
  • In case of a tie in correct answers, the team with the prompt and result closest to the original receives more marks.
  • Participants can only use the provided Adobe Firefly AI model after answering all 10 questions.
  • The decisions of the judges and organizers are final.

Tie Breaker Round (if needed)

    In case of a tie in correct answers, the team with the prompt and result most closely matching the original receives higher marks.
