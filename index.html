About the Event

Interface is an inter-collegiate technical fest held annually for over 25 years by the Department of Computer Science, CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Bangalore. Helmed by students of the Department of Computer Science, it aims to be at the forefront of innovation and collaboration, with new ideas and events being presented each year.

F.A.Q

  • What is Interface?

    Interface is a National-level Intercollegiate under-graduate IT festival organized by the Department of Computer Science, CHRIST (Deemed to be University) every year.

  • Can Post graduate students participate?

    No, this fest is meant exclusively for BCA and BSc students. Btech students are not eligible.

  • What is the registration fees?

    Registration Fees: ₹200/- per head. If a student is paying ₹200, they can take part in any number of events, Keeping in mind no two registered events by you clash.

  • Important Dates

    25th January 2024

  • Is there a dress code?

    Formals only.
